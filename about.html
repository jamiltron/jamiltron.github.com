About Me

My name is Justin Hamilton. I am currently a graduate student at the University of Nebraska at Omaha. I hold of Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Mathematics from there. I am also a full-time programmer for the United States Air Force Weather Agency.

I live with my wonderful girlfriend in Omaha, NE, and in my free time I enjoy practicing Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, reading, and playing games.
