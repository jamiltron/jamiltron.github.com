About Me

Hello, my name is Justin Hamilton. I am currently a graduate student at the University of Nebraska at Omaha. I hold of B.S. in Computer Science and Mathematics from there. I am also a full-time software engineer for a great Venture company, doing a lot of really awesome web and mobile development.

I am really interested in functional programming, having fell in love with Scheme when I discovered SICP, and later moved on to programming more and more in Clojure and Haskell, although I still have a lot of love for languages like Python, Ruby, and Lua.

I live with my wonderful girlfriend in Omaha, NE, and in my free time I enjoy practicing Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, reading, and playing games.
