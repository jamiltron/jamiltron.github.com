About Me

Hello, my name is Justin Hamilton. I am currently a graduate student at the University of Nebraska at Omaha. I hold of B.S. in Computer Science and Mathematics from there. I am also a full-time programmer for Prairie Ventures.

My current projects include a Forth interpreter written in Scheme and working through the problems listed at 4clojure. I also want to become better at functional programming (and Clojure in general) as well as try my hand at a web app or two.

I live with my wonderful girlfriend in Omaha, NE, and in my free time I enjoy practicing Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, reading, and playing games.
